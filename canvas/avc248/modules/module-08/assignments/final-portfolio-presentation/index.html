Final Portfolio Presentation

Module 8 — Final Package

Description

This is the culminating project of the semester. You will record a polished 2-minute video presenting your portfolio to a potential employer or freelance client. This video represents you when you are not in the room — make it count.

Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment you will be able to:

  • Present your portfolio professionally, concisely, and with confidence
  • Show at least 3 projects with clear descriptions of goals, process, and outcome
  • Demonstrate consistent professional presentation skills developed throughout the semester

Steps

  1. Review the feedback from your midterm presentation. This version should be noticeably more polished, more concise, and more targeted.
  2. Choose at least 3 projects. On at least one, show process work.
  3. Write bullet points for each project: goals, your role, key decisions, software used. Use these as a guide, not a script.
  4. Set up OBS. Dress professionally. Neutral background, front lighting, camera at eye level.
  5. Record your 2-minute presentation. Introduce yourself briefly, walk through your work, end with your portfolio URL and contact info.
  6. Watch your recording before uploading. Would you hire this person?
  7. Upload to your school YouTube. Post the link on Discord.
  8. Submit both the YouTube link and the MP4 file here in Canvas.

Do Not Do These Things

  • Apologize for your work or say "this isn't very good"
  • Say "when I graduate" — speak as a professional
  • Go over 2 minutes
  • Record in a distracting environment
  • Show the OBS interface on screen
  • Record on your phone
