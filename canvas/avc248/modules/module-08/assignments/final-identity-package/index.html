Final Identity Package

Module 8 — Final Package

Description

Your identity package is the collection of all your self-promotion materials — resume, business card, leave-behind, and branded digital assets — unified by a consistent design system. This submission gathers everything into one polished package.

Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment you will be able to:

  • Demonstrate consistency across all identity deliverables
  • Refine individual pieces based on feedback received throughout the semester
  • Present a professional-quality self-promotion package ready for real use

Steps

  1. Collect your finalized: resume, business card (choose your strongest of the 3 concepts), leave-behind, and any additional branded pieces (LinkedIn header, YouTube banner, portfolio banner, etc.).
  2. Review each piece: does it look like it belongs to the same person? Are the typefaces, colors, and visual tone consistent?
  3. Make any final refinements based on feedback received throughout the semester.
  4. Compile everything into a single PDF presentation showing all the pieces together. Include mockup images if you have them.
  5. Upload the PDF here.

Example

When someone looks at your resume, picks up your business card, and visits your portfolio site, they should feel like they are encountering the same person — the same professional voice, the same visual language.
