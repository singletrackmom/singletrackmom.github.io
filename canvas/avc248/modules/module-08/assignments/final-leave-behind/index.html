Final Leave-Behind

Module 8 — Final Package

Description

Your leave-behind is part of your final identity package. This is the refined, finished version of the concept you developed in Module 3 — polished, production-ready, and fully consistent with your brand.

Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment you will be able to:

  • Refine a leave-behind concept based on peer and instructor feedback
  • Deliver a finished, print-ready or digitally polished leave-behind
  • Demonstrate consistent branding across multiple identity deliverables

Steps

  1. Review the feedback on your Leave-Behind Concept from Module 3.
  2. Make sure your final leave-behind uses the same color palette, typefaces, and visual language as your business card, resume, and portfolio.
  3. Finalize your design. If printing, prepare a print-ready PDF. If digital, prepare a high-resolution PDF or image.
  4. The GCC Library Media Center Makerspace can help with 3D printing if you designed a 3D leave-behind.
  5. Upload a photo or PDF of your final leave-behind here.

Example

Your final version should be significantly more polished than your initial concept — refined typography, tightened layout, and production-ready quality. Look back at the examples from Module 3 for reference.
