Your Portfolio / Reel Plan

Module 4 — Midterm Portfolio

Description

Your midterm presentation is done and you have feedback. Now build a concrete plan for your final portfolio — which pieces you will include, what needs to be refined, and how you will use your time for the second half of the semester.

Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment you will be able to:

  • Evaluate portfolio feedback and identify priority improvements
  • Create a week-by-week production schedule for final portfolio completion
  • Select the most appropriate platform and format for your work

Steps

  1. Review the feedback you received on your midterm presentation.
  2. Decide where your final portfolio will live: Behance, Adobe Portfolio, ArtStation, a custom site, or Vimeo/YouTube for reels.
  3. List each project for your final portfolio. For each: project name, what needs revision or addition, and a realistic deadline.
  4. Create a week-by-week schedule from now through the final presentation with specific project and task per week.
  5. Remember: each project needs a description covering goals, process, your role, and software used.
  6. You can use AI to help — list your projects and tasks, give it your start and end dates, and ask it to create a timeline.
  7. Submit your plan as a text entry or PDF here.

Example

Example AI prompt for this assignment:

"I need to finish 4 portfolio projects by May 10. Here are the projects and what I still need to do for each: [list]. Please create a realistic week-by-week schedule starting March 23."

How to Submit

Submit as a text entry or attach a PDF.
