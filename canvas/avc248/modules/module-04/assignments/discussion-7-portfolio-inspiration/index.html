Discussion 7: Portfolio Inspiration

Module 4 — Midterm Portfolio

Description

The creative industry changes fast. AI tools, new platforms, shifting client budgets, and studio consolidations can all affect your job prospects. Staying current is not optional — it is part of being a professional.

Learning Objectives

  • Research current trends or events in your specific creative field
  • Analyze how an industry development might affect your career plans
  • Practice the habit of following industry news as a professional

Discussion Prompt

What is one thing happening in your industry right now that every student in this class should know about?

Instructions

  1. Read an article, watch a video, or listen to a podcast from the past 30 days about something happening in your field. Good places to look: Animation World Network, Cartoon Brew, Motionographer, 80 Level, or industry newsletters on Substack.
  2. Post in Discord: the article or source, a 3-4 sentence summary, and one sentence on how this affects students entering the field now.
  3. Comment on 3 classmates' posts — engage with the substance, not just 'thanks for sharing.'
  4. Compile your post and 3 comments in Miro and export as JPG.

Example

"Sony Pictures Animation just announced they are expanding their internal shorts program to give junior animators more development opportunities. [Cartoon Brew link]. This is significant because it means more entry-level creative positions at major studios rather than just tech/support roles."
