Big Interview Mock Job Interview

Module 7 — Interview Skills

Description

The Big Interview platform gives you a realistic recorded mock job interview in your field. You will answer questions on video one at a time and your responses will be graded on a rubric. Past students consistently find this one of the most valuable assignments in the course — treat it like a real interview.

Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment you will be able to:

  • Demonstrate professional interview conduct, body language, and communication
  • Apply the STAR method to answer behavioral interview questions
  • Reflect critically on your own interview performance

Steps

  1. Have a specific job in mind before you start. Review that job's requirements.
  2. Create your Big Interview account at gccaz.biginterview.com using code: GCCMARICOPA.
  3. Click Assignments in the top menu. Your assignment code is cea436.
  4. Read the time limit and take instructions, then click I'm ready!
  5. Click the interviewer video to hear each question, then click Start Recording. Wait for the countdown. Click Stop Recording when done.
  6. Review and re-record if takes remain. When satisfied, click Submit Assignment then Submit final answers.
  7. Write a reflection here (3-5 sentences): Was the software easy to use? Were you prepared? What would you do differently next time?

Interview Day Checklist

  • Professional attire — dress as if it is real
  • Neutral, uncluttered background
  • Good front lighting
  • Portfolio accessible on a second screen or tablet
  • Eye contact with the camera, not the screen

Example

Watch this walkthrough before you begin: Big Interview walkthrough (YouTube).

STAR Method reminder: Situation → Task → Action → Result. Use it especially for behavioral questions.

How to Submit

Complete your interview on Big Interview. Write your reflection in the Canvas text box.
