Mock Job Interview

Module 7 — Interview Skills

Description

This interview uses a Canva slide deck — one question per slide — to simulate a real job interview. Unlike the Big Interview platform, this version requires you to manage your own pacing. It is a more realistic simulation of a live video interview.

Learning Objectives

By completing this assignment you will be able to:

  • Demonstrate professional interview conduct in a self-directed format
  • Answer interview questions naturally without over-rehearsing
  • Apply STAR method responses to behavioral interview questions

Steps

  1. Have a specific job in mind before you begin.
  2. Open OBS. Make sure your face is visible in the bottom-right corner.
  3. Open the interview deck: Design Self Promotion Mock Interview (Canva).
  4. Each slide contains one question. Answer it, then advance. Do not rehearse — let answers come naturally. In a real interview you will not know the questions in advance.
  5. Have your portfolio accessible on a tablet, phone, or second screen.
  6. Watch your recording after you finish — note your pacing, eye contact, and how you came across.
  7. Upload to your school YouTube. Submit the URL and a brief reflection (2-3 sentences: Was this valuable? What would you do differently?).

Example

Watch this walkthrough before you start: Mock interview walkthrough (YouTube).

Remember the STAR method: Situation → Task → Action → Result. Use it especially on "Tell me about a time when..." questions.
