Discussion 1: Career Services Overview

Module 1 — Getting Started

Description

Before you can market yourself effectively, you need to know what you are marketing yourself for. This discussion combines a Career Services overview with your first reflection on professional goals — one of the most important things you will do this semester.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify at least two actionable takeaways from a career services presentation
  • Articulate a vision of your professional goals after graduation
  • Practice giving and receiving constructive peer feedback

Discussion Prompt

After watching the Career Services Overview, what is one thing you learned — and what does your dream job actually look like?

Instructions

  1. Watch the Career Services Overview video. Take notes.
  2. Post on Discord in #avc248-design-self-promotion: (1) two specific things you learned, and (2) a description of your dream job — be as specific as possible. What industry? What kind of company? What would a typical workday look like?
  3. Comment on at least 4 classmates' posts. Ask a follow-up question or share something you have in common.
  4. Compile your post and 4 comments into a single Miro frame. Export as JPG.

Example

A strong post goes beyond "I want to be an animator." Aim for this level of specificity:

"My dream job is working as a 3D character animator at a mid-size game studio like Insomniac or Naughty Dog. I'd love to specialize in facial animation for cinematic cutscenes. I'm open to relocating to LA or Seattle. Short term I'd take a junior position anywhere that does high-quality character work."
