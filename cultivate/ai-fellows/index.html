About the Program

Program Overview

The AI Fellows Summer Bootcamp is an eight-week interactive professional development experience designed for leaders in community and technical colleges who are responsible for advancing AI initiatives. The program provides participants with practical knowledge, hands-on experience, and a collaborative network to support meaningful and responsible AI adoption across their institutions.

Through expert-led sessions, guided project work, and peer collaboration, participants design and implement a real-world AI initiative tailored to their campus needs.

Program components

  • Expert-led weekly sessions
  • Guided project work on a real campus AI initiative
  • Structured peer collaboration across the cohort
  • Capstone project tailored to the fellow's institution

Why This Bootcamp

Why I Applied

My professional work sits at the intersection of learning experience design, applied AI, and student success. The AI Fellows cohort pairs that work with a cross-institutional peer network and structured expert input, which is hard to manufacture on a single campus. A bootcamp where every fellow is also building something real is the right fit for where I am, and the capstone structure gives me a container to move one of my existing projects forward with accountability and feedback.
