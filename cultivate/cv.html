Research & Professional Interests
Work focused on closing the gap between learning and real-world practice: in curriculum, in tools, and in the systems that support faculty and students. Spans instructional design, UX, graphic design, career readiness, and AI integration. Graduate research in connectivism and personal learning environments тАФ a framework since applied to building AI-powered tools that let faculty and students construct and tend their own learning infrastructure. Particularly interested in how AI changes what is possible in learning design, not just as a content tool but as a thinking partner, and in prompt engineering as a competency that should mirror industry demand.
