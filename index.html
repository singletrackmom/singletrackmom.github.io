Cultivate

Render

A student-facing career readiness tool designed for community college Digital Media Arts students. Render will surface curated AI tools for portfolio development, job search strategy, and post-graduation planning — meeting students where they are and scaffolding the transition from coursework to career. Developed in partnership with GCC Career Services.
